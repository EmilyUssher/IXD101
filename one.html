Follow the rhythm

For the 'Follow the rhythm' task, I went with a song by 'The Weeknd'. I chose this song because the aesthetics, the retro 80s style of rhythm and the music video itself has this same style.

'Blinding Lights' is an ode to love lost. The synth-heavy song is about someone who misses the romantic and physical intimacy with their ex.
